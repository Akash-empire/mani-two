AUXI Gen AI for PPT Design Workflow

To improve productivity and consistency within the PPT design team, I researched AUXI Gen AI, an AI-powered PowerPoint assistant, and proposed it as a supporting tool to simplify slide creation and content structuring.

AUXI Gen AI works directly inside PowerPoint, helping designers convert raw content into well-structured slides. By integrating this tool into the workflow, the team could reduce time spent on repetitive formatting and focus more on visual design, layout quality, and storytelling.

Problem Statement

The PPT design team frequently faced challenges such as:

  • High time spent on structuring slide content
  • Repetitive layout and formatting tasks
  • Inconsistent slide hierarchy across large decks
  • Tight deadlines for business and client presentations

These issues slowed down delivery and reduced time available for creative improvements.

Research & Analysis

I conducted research on multiple AI-based presentation tools and evaluated them based on:

  • Ease of use for designers
  • Compatibility with PowerPoint workflows
  • Ability to structure content logically
  • Design support without replacing creative control

AUXI AI stood out because it works inside PowerPoint and assists users by:

  • Structuring content into clear slide narratives
  • Maintaining consistency across slides
  • Supporting faster deck creation without disrupting design freedom

Proposed Solution

I proposed AUXI AI as a supporting PPT tool for the design team—not as a replacement for designers, but as an efficiency enabler.

The proposal focused on:

  • Using AUXI AI to generate structured slide outlines
  • Helping designers quickly convert raw content into presentation-ready slides
  • Reducing time spent on repetitive formatting
  • Improving overall slide consistency across projects

Impact on PPT Team Workflow

After evaluation and internal discussion, AUXI AI was positioned to:

  • Simplify early-stage slide creation
  • Speed up turnaround time for large decks
  • Allow designers to focus more on visuals, layout, and storytelling
  • Improve collaboration between content creators and designers

Key Takeaways

  • Research-driven tool selection can significantly improve team efficiency
  • AI works best as a design support tool, not a design replacement
  • Smart tooling allows designers to spend more time on creativity and quality
