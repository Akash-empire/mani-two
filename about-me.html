About Me

I’m Manikandan — a Senior Art Director and Graphic Designer with 8+ years of experience crafting brand-led design solutions for global audiences.

I work at the intersection of strategy, creativity, and execution, translating business goals into visually powerful, audience-focused stories. My experience spans B2B global brands across EMEA, APAC, and AMER, where I’ve led integrated campaigns across digital, print, motion, and presentation design.

Currently, I lead and mentor design teams, ensuring brand consistency, creative excellence, and scalable design systems. I enjoy solving complex problems, streamlining workflows, and using emerging tools — including AI-assisted design — to work smarter and faster without compromising quality.

I believe good design is not just about how it looks, but how it works, communicates, and delivers impact.
