Kepler.gl Map Design

Client

Global Logistics & Supply Chain Brand

Project Overview

The Kepler.gl Map Design project was part of a global DHL campaign that required visually consistent, data-accurate maps across multiple geographies. The client requested 72 total map assets—including 6 regional maps and 66 country-level maps—each delivered in two size formats for flexible usage across digital and presentation platforms.

The primary constraint was an extremely tight turnaround time, combined with the fact that the design team had no prior production experience using Kepler.gl, a powerful but complex geospatial analysis tool. Despite these challenges, the expectation remained uncompromised: high design quality, data accuracy, and brand consistency across all deliverables.

Client Challenge

  • Create regional and country-level map visuals within a short turnaround time.
  • Maintain high visual quality and consistency across 72 assets.
  • Quickly adapt to Kepler.gl, a new geospatial tool unfamiliar to the team.
  • Deliver outputs in multiple sizes without compromising accuracy or design standards.

Our Solution

To meet the aggressive timeline, the team adopted a rapid learning and execution approach. We quickly onboarded Kepler.gl by studying its data handling, layer controls, and export capabilities. Parallelly, we mapped out a streamlined workflow that connected data visualization → design refinement → quality control.

By leveraging Kepler.gl for accurate base-map generation and combining it with Adobe design tools for refinement, we ensured the outputs met both technical accuracy and DHL brand standards. This hybrid approach allowed us to scale production efficiently without sacrificing visual quality.

Design Process

1. Data Preparation & Tool Upskilling
  • Reviewed and validated country-level datasets provided by the client.
  • Rapidly trained on Kepler.gl functionalities, including layer styling, geographic filtering, and export settings.
2. Base Map Generation
  • Created accurate regional and country-level base maps directly in Kepler.gl.
  • Ensured geographic correctness, labeling clarity, and optimal zoom levels for each map.
3. Design Refinement & Branding
  • Imported exported maps into Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • Applied DHL brand colors, typography alignment, and visual hierarchy.
  • Standardized visual treatments to maintain consistency across all 72 assets.
4. Size Adaptation & Quality Control
  • Developed two size variations for each map to support multiple use cases.
  • Conducted internal QC checks to verify data accuracy, alignment, and export consistency.

Results

  • Delivered 72 map assets (6 regional + 66 country-level) within the committed deadline.
  • Maintained 100% consistency across maps despite high volume and multiple size formats.
  • Ensured smooth stakeholder coordination through direct project ownership and clear communication.
  • Enabled the client to deploy maps seamlessly across campaign materials without rework.

Client Feedback

“Excellent service on the graphic illustration requests, very good ideas, and easy alignment with the team.”

The client appreciated the quality of output and speed of delivery, especially given the complexity and scale of the task.

Key Takeaways

  • Rapid upskilling on new tools can significantly reduce dependency and turnaround time.
  • A structured, repeatable workflow is essential for managing high-volume design production.
  • Combining data-driven tools with traditional design software delivers both accuracy and brand excellence.
  • Strong ownership and coordination play a key role in meeting tight deadlines at scale.

Key Takeaways

  • AI tools can significantly reduce costs when applied thoughtfully within a design workflow.
  • Combining AI generation with professional design refinement ensures enterprise-quality output.
  • Innovation in tooling strengthens creative problem-solving and client confidence.
  • AI-driven design systems can scale efficiently across large presentation programs.
