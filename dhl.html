DHL– Global Art Direction & Brand Governance

1. Role Overview

Position: Art Director

Account: DHL Global

Scope: End-to-end creative leadership across all DHL global divisions — Supply Chain, Consulting, E-Commerce, Post & Paket, Customer Solutions & Innovation.

Responsibility: Lead the visual strategy, brand governance, and design delivery for global campaigns, sales & service decks, and communication assets.

Summary:
As the Art Director managing the DHL account, I ensure brand-aligned, high-quality visual outputs across multiple divisions and regions. My role encompasses creative leadership, problem-solving, team guidance, and strategic design oversight, ensuring all deliverables maintain brand consistency and communicate effectively across global audiences.

2. Account Challenges

  • Managing multi-division design requests with varied objectives and formats.
  • Ensuring visual and brand consistency across Supply Chain, Consulting, E-Commerce, Post & Paket, and Customer Solutions & Innovation divisions.
  • Translating complex business and sales requirements into clear, persuasive visual communication.
  • Handling fast turnaround times for Sales & Service decks and high-priority campaigns.
  • Coordinating multiple internal teams (design, QC, client services) and stakeholders globally.

3. Objectives

  • Deliver visually engaging, brand-compliant designs for all DHL divisions.
  • Standardize layout, illustration, and visual hierarchy across global campaigns.
  • Solve design challenges proactively, ensuring clarity and consistency.
  • Support sales and service enablement through high-quality decks and presentations.
  • Reduce revisions and ensure first-time-right delivery across all divisions.

4. My Approach / Strategy

1. Brand Governance & Global Consistency

  • Maintain DHL’s global brand standards across divisions and regions.
  • Define visual rules for typography, color, illustration, and layout.
  • Ensure all assets (campaigns, decks, reports) reflect DHL’s brand integrity.

2. Creative Problem-Solving

  • Analyze briefs and sales/service requirements at a strategic level.
  • Identify potential risks to clarity, brand consistency, and usability.
  • Provide proactive solutions before client review.

3. Art Direction & Team Leadership

  • Guide designers across divisions, ensuring quality and consistency.
  • Review assets in-progress and provide constructive feedback.
  • Mentor team members on brand rules and visual decision-making.
  • Align design outputs with global sales and service deck standards.

5. Key Deliverables

  • Marketing and campaign assets across divisions:
  • Marketing and campaign assets across divisions:
      a) Supply Chain
      b) Consulting
      c) E-Commerce
      c) Post & Paket
      c) Customer Solutions & Innovation
  • Visual guidelines, templates, and presentation frameworks
  • Consistently high-quality digital and print-ready files

6. Outcomes & Impact

  • Achieved high first-time approval rates across all divisions.
  • Reduced revision cycles and turnaround times for multi-region projects.
  • Strengthened client trust by delivering visually consistent, brand-compliant assets.
  • Demonstrated strategic creative leadership for global DHL communications.
  • Positioning as the trusted point of contact for multi-division design strategy.

7. Business & Brand Impact

  • Protected and strengthened DHL’s global brand identity.
  • Ensured clear, consistent messaging across all global divisions.
  • Supported sales and service enablement through professional, brand-aligned decks.
  • Enabled the design team to scale efficiently across multiple divisions.

8. Key Takeaways

  • Managing a global account requires strategic art direction, not just visual execution.
  • Brand consistency is critical across multiple divisions and formats.
  • Effective problem-solving in design ensures clarity, efficiency, and client confidence.
  • Art direction adds tangible business value by aligning visuals with global strategy.
